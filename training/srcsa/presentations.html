Survivor Receiving Center and Staging Areas: Presentations

Available in OpenDocument or Adobe Acrobat Format!

As part of the SRCSA guidebook release we are also releasing a presentation that provides an overview of the SRCSA concept and how it is designed to function. This presentation would work well as an introduction for those who may not be familiar with the SRCSA concept, or an understanding of how the centers are designed to work.

As with all of our educational materials, this presentation is available in both the OpenDocument presentation (opd) format as well in Adobe Acrobat (pdf) format. You can view all the file releases on our download page or simply download the presentation in Open Document or Adobe Acrobat format.
