EMS Lesson Plans

Available in OpenDocument or Adobe Acrobat Format!

In this section our intent is to create lesson plans that you can use and modify for use in the classroom. At this time we are not listing the individual lessons, but instead want to keep the subject area as open as possible. We do not want anyone to feel that their particular lesson of skill is not wanted simply because it is not listed. As these lessons are created, they will be added on this page.

The lesson plans created here will be the basis for the presentations that are developed either concurrently or at a later time. However, it is recommended that the lesson plan be created first, before the presentation.

If you are interested in helping to create lesson plans for the project, please be sure to visit our Resources page. Here you will find the guidelines for developing the lesson plans, as well as templates to help you out. Also, make sure you sign up for an account so that we can list you as a contributor to the project.
